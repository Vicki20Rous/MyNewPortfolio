About Victoria

Hello! My name is Victoria Rouser. I am a highly motivated & career-driven Entry-Level Full Stack Web Developer. I started my web development journey in 2020 through the Lifelong & Professional Education Department of Kansas University (Rock Chalk JayHawk!!) & received Certification in Full Stack Web Development. During my time with KU Bootcamp, I received knowledge of programming languages such as JavaScript, CSS, HTML, ExpressJS, NodeJS & React. Also, I was educated about database management programs like MongoDB & MySQL. Upon those web developing studies, I also learned how to structure a webpage using the MERN method.

Enthused about pursuing a new profession in web development, I decided to improve my credentials by enrolling with LaunchCode LC101 to further my knowledge with JavaScript. While bettering my JavaScript intellect, LaunchCode as taught me the programming language Java. With their great guidance & support I was able to complete & graduate from their program.

During my spare time my hobbies include reading books, gardening, playing volleyball & visiting museums. Fun Facts about me that always surprises people, I'm a big fan of Marvel / DC Comics!!! I'm growing a love for golf as well as shooting pool also I'm a huge football fan (Chiefs Kingdom!!)
